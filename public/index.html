Easy to Use Commands

Basic Commands

/join Join your voice channel
/leave Leave the voice channel
/say [text] Make the bot speak in voice chat
/test Test your announcement phrases

Customization

/phrase join [message] Set custom join phrase
/phrase leave [message] Set custom leave phrase
/voice set [voice] Choose your TTS voice
/voice speed [50-200] Adjust speech speed

Quick Setup Guide

1

Invite VoiceBot

Click the "Add to Discord" button and select your server. VoiceBot needs permissions to join voice channels and send messages.

2

Join a Voice Channel

Use /join to make VoiceBot join your current voice channel. It will automatically start announcing user activity.

3

Customize Your Experience

Set custom phrases with /phrase, choose your voice with /voice set, and adjust speed to your liking.

4

Test & Enjoy

Use /test to hear your custom phrases, then enjoy seamless voice channel announcements!
